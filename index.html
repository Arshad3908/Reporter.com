THE PROBLEMS WE SEE

Ignored Issues

Potholes, broken streetlights, and drainage problems go unreported for months. Communities suffer in silence while problems worsen.

Complex Processes

Reporting local problems requires multiple phone calls, office visits, and paperwork. It's too complicated and time-consuming.

No Transparency

After reporting, there's no way to track progress. Citizens never know if their reports are being addressed or simply forgotten.

Weak Community Voice

Individual complaints lack impact. Without unified community support, important issues remain low priority for authorities.

Location Confusion

Describing exact problem locations over the phone is difficult. Authorities struggle to find reported issues without precise coordinates.

Delayed Response

Critical infrastructure problems that need immediate attention take weeks to address, causing safety risks and community frustration.

POWERFUL FEATURES

01

Photo Documentation

Upload photos to show exactly what needs fixing. AI automatically analyzes images and suggests problem categories, making reporting faster and more accurate.

02

GPS Precision

Pinpoint exact locations with interactive maps. One-tap geolocation or manual pin placement ensures authorities find the problem without confusion.

03

Community Voting

Vote on reported problems to show community priority. High-voted issues get attention faster, giving citizens collective bargaining power.

04

Comments & Updates

Join discussions about local issues. Get notifications when problems are addressed. Build community dialogue around shared concerns.

05

Smart Search

Find similar issues in your area before reporting duplicates. Search by category, location, or keywords to join existing discussions.

06

Privacy Control

Choose to make your email public or private. Participate openly or anonymously while maintaining accountability through verified accounts.

HOW IT WORKS

01

Snap & Report

Take a photo of the problem. Our AI analyzes it and suggests the right category. Add a description, pin the location on the map, and submit in under 60 seconds.

02

Community Support

Your neighbors vote on the issue to show it matters. Comment with additional details. The more community support, the faster the response from authorities.

03

Watch Progress

Track your report's status in real-time. Get notified when it moves from "Reported" to "In Progress" to "Resolved". See the impact of your voice.
